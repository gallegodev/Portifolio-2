Anubis Game


Once every 100 years, the god Anubis selects mortals from around the world to compete in a deadly Tik Tak Toe challenge.


Those who lose the challenge will give their souls to the winners who will grow wiser, and stronger and receive all the remaining years of the defeated challenger.


However to win the game you have to obey the rules:


  1. The game is played on a 3 square by 3 square grid.
  2. You are X, and the other challenger is O. Players take turns placing their marks on empty squares.
  3. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across or diagonally) is the winner.
  4. When all 9 squares are full, the game ends. If no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.


Praise be the strongest challenger!
